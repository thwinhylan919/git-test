US ORIGINATIONS

Oracle Banking Digital Experience Retail Originations has been enhanced to comply with following areas of US regulations and disclosures:

    • State of Residence
    • Eligibility Check
    • Account Funding
    • Regulations, Disclosures, and Consents

US origination localization dashboard allows you to open following account application:

    • Certificate of Deposit Application
    • Checking Account Application
    • Savings Account Application
    • Application Tracker

To open an account:

  1. Open an internet browser to access the application.
  2. Type the Oracle Banking Digital Experience URL in the Address bar, and press Enter. The application dashboard appears.
  3. Click appropriate card to open a new account. The respective account opening screen appears.
