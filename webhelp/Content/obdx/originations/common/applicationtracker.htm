Application Tracker

You can see the complete details / features of the selected credit card and proceed further with the credit card application process. The orientation of credit card helps you to understand, the application process, time required, and the documents required to complete the application submission.

How to reach here:

Dashboard > Credit CardA credit card is a payment card issued to users (cardholders) as a method of payment. > Credit Card Origination > Application Tracker

To track credit card application:

  1. Click the desired card in the My Application. The card details appear with the status and pending actions.
  2. In the Pending Actions section, click Additional Preferences.
    The Card Preferences, Balance Transfer, and Membership Details section appears.

  1. If you click Card Preferences:
  2. Click Upload your image to upload a image.
  3. Click Select a Name on card, to select the name to appear on the card.
  4. Select the background image for the card.
  5. If you click Balance Transfer:
  6. Select Yes / No if you want to transfer previous balance.
  7. In the Promo Code field, enter the promotional code for the card.
  8. From the Card Issuer Name list, select the card issuer name.
  9. In the Payee Name field, enter the name of the payee.
  10. In the Card Number field, enter the card number.
  11. In the Transfer Amount field, enter the amount to be transferred.
  12. To accept the terms and conditions for balance transfer, select the I agree to the terms and conditions for balance transfercheck box.
  13. If you click Membership Details:
  • From the Type of Membership list, select the membership type.
  • In the Membership Number field, enter the number corresponding to the membership type.
  • Select Yes / No if you wish to receive notifications about the increase in credit limit.
  • Select Yes / No if you have business relations with other bank.
  • Click Continue. The Documents section appears.
  1. Click to upload the document. For more information on uploading a document click here.
  2. In the Tracking Dashboard screen, click Applicationto view the credit card application, primary card, and add on card summary.

    OR
    Click Status History to view the card application history. For more information click here.
    OR
    Click Cancel Application to abort the card application process. For more information click here.
