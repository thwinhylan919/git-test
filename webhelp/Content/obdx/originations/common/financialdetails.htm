Financial Details

The personal details are the primary information about the account holder. It includes following details:

  • Income
  • Expense
  • Assets
  • Liabilities
  • Register User

To add asset / liability details:

  1. From the Type of Asset list, select the appropriate option.
  2. In the Value field, enter the value of the liability in the given currency .
  3. In the Ownership (%) field, enter the percentage of ownership that the applicant has on the asset.
  4. Click Save.

Note: To add asset details, click button.

  1. From the Type of Liability list, select the appropriate option.
  2. In the Value field, enter the value of the liability.
  3. In the Outstanding Value field, enter the outstanding value of the liability.
  4. From the Frequency of Expense list, select the expense frequency.
  5. In the Ownership (%) field, enter the percentage of ownership that the applicant has on the liability.
  6. Click Save.

Note: To add liability details, click button.

To add expense / income details:

  • From the Income Type list, select the income source of the applicant.
  • In the Share of Income (%) field, enter the percentage of applicants income.
  • In the Gross income ($) field, enter the applicants gross income.
  • In the Net Income ($) field, enter the applicants net income.
  • From the Frequency list, select the income frequency.
  • Click Save to update the income details.

Note: To add new income details, click button.

  1. In the Expense section, from the Type of expense list, select the applicants expense.
  2. In the Share of expense (%) field, enter the percentage of applicants expense.
  3. In the Total expense value ($) field, enter the applicants expense value.
  4. From the Frequency list, select the expense frequency.

Note: To add new expense details, click button.

To register a user

  • The Register section appears. From the Salutation list, select the applicants name prefix.
  • In the First Name field, enter the applicants first name.
  • In the Last Name field, enter the applicants last name.
  • From the Gender list, select the applicants gender.
  • In the Date field, select the birth date of the applicant.
  • In the Email field, enter the email address.

  • In the Enter Password field, enter the password

  • To enable the terms and conditions, select the Accept Terms and Conditions check box.
