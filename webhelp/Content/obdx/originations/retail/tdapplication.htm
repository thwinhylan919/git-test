Term Deposit Application

Term deposit application allows you to apply for a term deposit account. Term deposit account opening application goes through a particular cycle. Following are the steps involved in the account opening application:

  • Orientation: It is the summary view of the term deposit application process which you have to follow to open a term deposit account.
  • Basic Details: Basic details of the term deposit such as the deposit amount, interest payout frequency, and tenure.
  • Confirmation: It is the agreement before starting the account opening application process.
  • Application: You have to specify the personal and professional details.
  • Review: Summary of the term deposit application after selecting an offer.
  • Approval: Successful submission of the term deposit application.

How to reach here:

Dashboard > New Deposit

To apply for a term deposit account:

  1. Click Apply. The Orientation screen appears.
    OR
    Click Back to navigate to the previous screen.
  1. Click I am Ready.
  2. The Term Deposit Requirement screen appears. In the Deposit Amount field, enter the amount to be deposited.
  3. From the Interest Payout Frequency list, select the interest frequency to be paid out.
  4. From the Tenure Years list, select the tenure in years of the term deposit.
  5. From the Months list, select the tenure in months of the term deposit.
  6. From the Co-applicant list, select the number of co-applicants for the term deposit.
  7. If you select one or more than one co-applicants, from the Co-applicant Relationship list, select the relationship with the co-applicant.
  8. To accept the terms and conditions, select the I accept the Terms and Conditions check box.
  1. Click Apply.
  2. The Confirmation screen appears. Verify the details and click Confirm. The Application screen appears.
    OR
    Click Edit to modify the entered details.
  3. In the Primary Information, Contact Information, Identity Information, and Occupation Information section, enter the appropriate details. For more information click here.

Note: If there is one or more co-applicants repeat step 12 to the enter the primary information, contact information, identity information, and occupation information.
