Add Biller

Customer can pay the utility bills to their utility vendors through Bill payment only post maintenance of a biller through Add Biller. Below details are captured during biller maintenance.

  • Category to which the vendor associates.
  • Biller name
  • Relationship No : Relationship Number given by the vendor

This option also allow you to initiate a payment to the added biller.

How to reach here:

Payments Dashboard > Manage Billers > Add New Biller

To register a biller:

  • All the register billers appears on Manage Billers screen.

Add Biller

Field Description

Field Name

Description

Category

Category of the biller.

Biller Name

Name of registered biller.

Relationship No

Relationship number of the customer with the biller.
  1. From the Category list, select the appropriate category of the biller.
  2. From the Biller Name list, select the appropriate registered biller to make bill payment.
  3. In the Relationship No field, enter the relationship number of the customer with the biller.
  4. Click Add.
  5. The Review screen appears. Verify the details, and click Confirm .
    OR
    Click Cancel to cancel the transaction.
  6. The Verification screen appears if transaction is configured for OTP validation. For more information click here.
  7. The success message appears.
    Click Done to complete the transaction.
    OR
    To initiate a payment to the added biller, click Pay.

Note: For more information on managing billers, click here.

FAQs

How do I know that my registration request for a biller has been processed?

You will receive an success message confirming the registration of your biller/s. You can begin paying your bills only after receipt of such a confirmation.
