Manage Biller

Customer can pay the utility bills to their utility vendors through Bill payment only post maintenance of a biller.

Using this option, you can register biller to make bill payment to the registered biller. You can add / modify / delete the biller. This option also allow you to initiate a payment to the added biller.

How to reach here:

Payments Dashboard > Manage Billers

To manage a biller:

  • All the register biller card appears on Manage Billers screen.

Modify Biller

Using this option, you can edit the biller details.

To modify biller:

  • Click on relevant biller card whose details you want to modify.
  • The Biller Details screen appears. Click Edit.
  • The Edit Biller screen appears. Modify the biller registration details.
  • Click Edit.
    OR
    Click Cancel to cancel the transaction.
  • The Review screen appears. Verify the details, and click Confirm .
    OR
    Click Cancel to cancel the transaction.
  • The success message appears.
    Click Done to complete the transaction.
    OR
    To initiate a payment to the added biller, click Pay.

Delete Biller

Using this option, you can delete the registered biller.

To delete biller:

  • Click on relevant biller card whom you want to delete.
  • The Biller Details screen appears. Click .
  • The message for confirmation to delete biller appears on Delete Biller screen. Click Delete Biller to delete the biller.
    OR
    Click Cancel to cancel the transaction.
  • The success message appears.
    Click Done to complete the transaction.

FAQs

Can I cancel my biller registration request?

Yes, you can cancel your registration request for a biller by using the "Delete Biller" option.
