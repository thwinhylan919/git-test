LOG-IN & LOG-OUT OF ORACLE BANKING DIGITAL EXPERIENCE

Log-in to the application

You require the authenticate credentials to log in to the Oracle Banking Digital Experience application.

To log in to the application:

  1. Open an internet browser to access the application.
  2. Type the Oracle Banking Digital Experience URL in the Address bar, and press Enter.
  1. The Oracle Banking Digital Experience home page appears. Click Login.
  2. The Login screen appears. In the Username field, enter the user ID.
  3. In the Password field, enter the password.

Note: The characters typed in the Password field appear masked (••••••) for security reasons.

  1. Click Login.
  1. The Overview screen appears with broad level financial summary, outstanding and available balance in current and savings account, loans, term deposit, and credit cards.
