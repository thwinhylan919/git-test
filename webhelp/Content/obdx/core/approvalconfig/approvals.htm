APPROVALS

Approval management allows the bank administrator to configure approval rules for maintenances or transactions as per the requirement. Configuration is available to specify:

  • Whether approval is required or not for a particular transaction/maintenance.
  • Number of approval levels required (current support is up to 5 level of approval).
  • Whether the approval should be sequential or parallel.

Following maintenances are used to configure approval rules;

  • User Group Maintenance
  • Approval Workflow Maintenance
  • Approval Rule Maintenance
