ACCOUNTS

Application provides real time access to Current and Savings accounts functions. Customer can view balances, view account statements, initiate service requests and perform financial transactions on their accounts.

Various transactions available under the account module:

  • Account Overview
  • Account Summary
  • Account Details
  • Cheque Status Inquiry
  • Stop/Unblock Cheque
  • Cheque Book Request
  • Statement Request
